B.J. Habibie

B. J. Habibie's Life Timeline

  • 1936 - Born in Parepare, South Sulawesi
  • 1954 - Study Mechanical Engineering in Universitas Indonesia
  • 1955 - Study Aviation Engineering in RWTH Aechen, West German
  • 1960 - Received Engineer's degree with title Diplom-Ingeniur
  • 1965 - Received Engineer's degree with title Doktorengineur
  • 1974 - Soeharto, the second president of Indonesia, recruited Habibie to return to Indonesia as part of his drive to industrialize and develop the country
  • 1976 - Habibie was made CEO of the new-state owned enterprise Industri Pesawat Terbang Nurtanio, which in 1985 changed its name to Industri Pesawat Terbang Nusantara
  • 1998 - Suharto announced his resignation. Habibie, as the Constitution says, succeeded him as president.
